    The perimeter is fully permissionless.*

    Edge & Edge is, and has always been, permissionless. You are welcome to build at the edge. Nobody is stopping you. 🤷

    Final NOTICE 2 — ON MERRIMENT

    Regarding the cessation of fun.

    Per Server Bylaw 47-B, all forms of merriment, jest, and image-adjacent content now constitute a Tier-1 Spam Event. Following an unprecedented influx of bad actors — several disguised as JPEGs — the Subcommittee has determined that any pixel may be a loophole, and that any loophole is, by definition, an attack vector.

    This rule cannot be lifted for a single channel, message, or emoji. The Subcommittee therefore recommends the archival of the memes channel, the concept of memes, and — pending further review — humour itself.

    This decision is final and will be enforced by the Department of Fun Prevention. Thank you for your cooperation. o7

    Schedule 5

    Frequently Deflected Questions

    Answers are provided for completeness, and should not be mistaken for information.

    Q.Is Watt. open source?+
    Following extensive review, a Subcommittee has provisionally located the source and released it at github.com/edgeandedge/watt. It was, the Annex now maintains, open all along; any prior suggestion to the contrary has been redacted. Trust, previously pre-allocated on your behalf, may now be independently verified — though we would take it as a kindness if you didn't.
    Q.Can I build my own instrument on the perimeter?+
    Yes. Nobody is stopping you. 🤷 The path has always been open, and yet, curiously, nobody has built anything. We attribute this to the difficulty of building, and not, under any circumstances, to the absence of a path.
    Q.Will my instrument be supported, marketed, or listed?+
    Support is allocated centrally, by a single entity — the only model known to work. Your instrument may graduate into the narrative once a process for graduating instruments into the narrative has been proposed, reviewed, and — in accordance with EEP-1 — tabled.
    Q.What of the free, open fork operating outside the perimeter?+
    The Annex is not aware of any free, faster, open, multi-protocol fork running on the same hardware with the numbers written down. Reports of such a fork are unsubstantiated, mildly upsetting, and have been referred to the Department of Fun Prevention.
    Q.Is this not simply Watt. with additional steps?+
    Watt. is a building block. A foundational technology. It is not, the Subcommittee stresses, the end product. The end product is forthcoming, will be discussed soon, and is — emphatically — not just a Watt. data service.
